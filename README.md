# 🐾 Animal Forest Run - 3D 4K

A stunning 3D HTML5 game built with Three.js where you experience the forest in full 3D graphics with 4K resolution support! Start as a small mouse and evolve into bigger animals by eating different types of food in a beautiful 3D forest environment with realistic lighting, shadows, and immersive gameplay.

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Start Small**: Begin your journey as a tiny mouse 🐭
2. **Eat to Grow**: Collect different types of food to grow bigger
3. **Evolve**: Transform into different animals as you grow:
   - Mouse 🐭 → Rabbit 🐰 → Fox 🦊 → Deer 🦌 → Bear 🐻 → Elephant 🐘
4. **Avoid Obstacles**: Stay away from rocks and obstacles
5. **Score Points**: Different foods give different points

## 🍎 Food Types

- **🫐 Berries**: 10 points (smallest)
- **🍎 Apples**: 15 points
- **🥕 Carrots**: 20 points
- **🍄 Mushrooms**: 25 points
- **🌰 Acorns**: 30 points
- **🍯 Honey**: 40 points (highest)

## ⚡ Powerups (Traders Only)

- **💨 Fart Powerup**: 15 coins, enables flying for 15 seconds
- **🫧 Bubble Powerup**: 10 coins, extends swim time by 2 seconds
- **⚡ Speed Powerup**: 20 coins, increases speed for 3 seconds
- **❤️ Health Powerup**: 25 coins, restores 50 health points

*All powerups must be purchased from traders - they no longer spawn in the world*

## 🎯 Trading System

### 🛒 How to Trade
1. **Find Traders**: Look for golden raccoon merchants (🦝) or human traders (🛒)
2. **Click to Trade**: Click on any trader to open the shop interface
3. **Browse Categories**: 
   - ⚔️ Weapons & Combat: Swords, bows, and arrows
   - ⚡ Powerups & Effects: Temporary boosts and abilities
   - 🛡️ Items & Upgrades: Permanent stat improvements
4. **Purchase Items**: Click "Buy" to purchase with your coins
5. **Close Shop**: Click the red X in the top right or click outside the panel

### 💰 What You Can Buy
- **Weapons**: Swords for melee combat, bows and arrows for ranged combat
- **Powerups**: Temporary effects like flying, swimming, speed boosts, health restoration
- **Upgrades**: Permanent improvements to energy, health, swimming, speed, stomach capacity
- **Equipment Display**: See your current weapons and powerups in the shop interface

## 🎯 Controls

- **WASD** or **Arrow Keys**: Move your animal in 3D space
  - **W/Up**: Move forward in the direction you're facing
  - **S/Down**: Move backward
  - **A/Left**: Turn left
  - **D/Right**: Turn right
- **Mouse**: Look around the 3D environment
- **P**: Poop when your eyes turn red
- **S**: Sleep to regenerate energy (night only)
- **F**: Fly for 15 seconds (costs energy)
- **R**: Restart the game
- **Click "Play Again"**: Restart after game over

## 👁️ Eye Color System

- **Black Eyes**: Normal state
- **Red Eyes**: Need to poop (press P)
- **Green Eyes**: Hungry (find food to eat)
