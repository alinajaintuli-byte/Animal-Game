How to Play:

  • 🌤️ Experience the forest from a bird's eye view!
  • 🛤️ Follow the windy dirt trail through the forest
  • 🏁 Reach the finish line for speed-based coin rewards!
  • ⚠️ Stay on the trail - off-trail areas have wolves and hunters!
  • 🏹 Hunters shoot arrows at you when you're off the trail!
  • 🐺 Wolves hunt in packs of 4-6 - stay away from them!
  • ⚔️ Wolves and hunters deal direct health damage on contact!
  • 📉 Getting attacked makes you smaller and devolve!
  • 💀 Health reaches 0% = GAME OVER - you get nothing!
  • 🐭 Start as a mouse and evolve into bigger animals
  • 🍎 Eat different foods to grow and evolve
  • 🍊 Fruits provide extra health regeneration and growth!
  • 📈 You can only grow when at 100% health!
  • 💩 Press P when your eyes turn red to poop
  • 💤 Press S to sleep and restore energy
  • 🦅 Press F to fly for 5 seconds (costs energy)
  • 🪙 Collect coins to buy upgrades from traders
  • 🛒 Find merchant animals (🦝) and trader people (🛒) along the trail
  • 🦕 Giant Growth upgrade makes you 10x larger!
  • 🐿️ Green dots = friendly animals, Red dots = dangerous
  • 👥 Green dots = friendly people, Red dots = dangerous
  • 💨 Fart powerups let you fly for a limited time
  • 🫧 Bubble powerups extend your swimming time
  • 🌊 Some animals can swim in water areas
  • 🌙 Day/night cycle affects animal behavior
  • 🌧️ Weather affects your animal's needs
  • 👁️ Green eyes = hungry, need to eat!
  • ⚡ Manage energy, thirst, hunger, and health
  • 🏪 Use coins to buy upgrades and items
  • 🌲 Avoid obstacles and stay in the forest!
